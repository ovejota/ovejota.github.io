Hernán Biasotti – Sound, Editing, and Direction
I'm a sound designer, editor, and director in film, television and other media, with over 17 years of experience in the audiovisual industry.
I focus on shaping stories through sound and editing, always aiming to enhance narrative and immersion in each project. Throughout my career, I’ve collaborated on a wide range of productions, exploring various ways to integrate sound and image, including interactive experiences. As a director, I’ve led both film and TV projects, expanding my narrative approach across different areas of audiovisual creation.
